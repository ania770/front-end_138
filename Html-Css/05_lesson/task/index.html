What is Egg Car Racing?

Egg Car Racing combines the fun of racing with the challenge of keeping a delicate egg unbroken. Your job is to drive a wobbly car with an egg onboard through rough roads, making sure it doesn't crack. Every bump and obstacle presents a new challenge, requiring careful acceleration, braking, and jumping. Unlock Eggy Cars as you progress, and try your best to protect the egg at all costs!
